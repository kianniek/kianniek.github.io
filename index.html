Shift Exporter

Effortlessly convert your work schedules to Google Calendar

About the App

Shift Exporter is an Android application designed to simplify your work schedule management. Built with modern Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, the app seamlessly converts Excel spreadsheet (.xlsx) work schedules into Google Calendar events, ensuring you never miss a shift.

Key Features

  • Excel Schedule Import: Upload your work schedule directly from .xlsx files
  • Google Calendar Integration: Automatic sync with your Google Calendar
  • Smart Schedule Parsing: Intelligent recognition of shift patterns and types
  • Role Detection: Automatically identifies your role (Opener, Sluiter, etc.) based on shift timing
  • Colleague Tracking: See who's working with you and their schedules
  • Batch Processing: Preview all shifts before syncing with a single button
  • Professional Design: Clean, modern interface with blue and light gray color scheme

How It Works

  1. Sign In with Google
    Authenticate securely using your Google account to enable Calendar access
  2. Upload Your Schedule
    Select and upload your work schedule Excel file (.xlsx format)
  3. Automatic Parsing
    The app scans for your shifts and intelligently processes schedule details
  4. Preview & Verify
    Review all detected shifts with dates, times, and calculated roles
  5. Sync to Calendar
    One-click synchronization adds all shifts to your Google Calendar

Schedule Processing Details

The app uses sophisticated logic to accurately convert your shifts:

Shift Recognition

  • Searches for the name "Mahnoy" in the Excel file to identify your shifts
  • All events are titled "Werk Gollem" for easy identification in your calendar
  • Start times are extracted directly from your schedule

Smart End Time Calculation

  • Flex Shifts: Automatically set to end at 23:59
  • Closing Shifts ("sluit"):
    • Monday-Thursday & Sunday: Ends at 02:00 (next day)
    • Friday & Saturday: Ends at 04:00 (next day)
  • Regular Shifts: Use the time specified in the schedule

Role Assignment

  • Opener: Assigned if you have the earliest start time that day
  • Sluiter: Assigned for closing shifts where you're last to leave
  • Eerste aanwezig: Indicates your relative arrival order

Event Details

Each calendar event includes your role and a complete list of all colleagues working that day, along with their shift times, displayed in an easy-to-read format with line breaks between entries.

Data Usage & Privacy

Why We Request Google Calendar Access

This app requests access to your Google Calendar for one specific purpose: to create calendar events for your work shifts.

What we access:

  • Permission to add events to your Google Calendar
  • Your Google account authentication (via Google Sign-In)

What we DO NOT do:

  • We do not read your existing calendar events
  • We do not store your schedule data on external servers
  • We do not share your information with third parties
  • We do not sell or monetize your data

All processing happens locally on your Android device. Your Excel files and schedule information are processed entirely on your phone using Firebase for authentication only.

Technical Information

Platform: Android
Technology Stack: Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Firebase Authentication
APIs Used: Google Calendar API (for event creation only)
Data Storage: Local device only, no cloud storage
Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0 via Firebase
