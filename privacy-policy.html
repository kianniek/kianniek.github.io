Privacy Policy

App Name: Shift Exporter (com.kianhamidi.shiftexporter)

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Overview

Shift Exporter is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Android application to convert work schedules from Excel files into Google Calendar events.

Information Collection and Use

Our application accesses and processes the following information:

  • Excel Schedule Files: You upload .xlsx files containing your work schedule. These files are processed locally on your device only.
  • Google Account Information: We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your identity and obtain permission to access your Google Calendar.
  • Google Calendar Access: We request permission to create calendar events in your Google Calendar.

We do not store, share, or sell any of your personal information.

Google Calendar API Usage

This application uses the Google Calendar API specifically to:

  • Create new calendar events for your work shifts
  • Add event details including shift title ("Werk Gollem"), start/end times, and descriptions (role and colleague information)

We do NOT:

  • Read, access, or view your existing calendar events
  • Modify or delete your existing calendar events
  • Access any other Google services beyond Calendar event creation
  • Share your calendar data with any third parties

Data Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your Android device:

  • Excel files are parsed entirely on your device
  • Shift information is extracted and formatted on your device
  • Calendar events are created directly from your device to Google Calendar
  • No intermediate servers are used to process or store your schedule data

Data Storage

We do not operate any servers that store your data. Specifically:

  • Your Excel schedule files remain only on your device
  • We do not upload or transmit your schedule data to any external servers
  • Calendar events are stored solely in your Google Calendar (managed by Google)
  • Authentication tokens are managed by Firebase Authentication and Google

Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure authentication and authorization
  • Firebase Authentication: To facilitate Google Sign-In
  • Google Calendar API: To create calendar events

These services are governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Calendar events are sent directly from your device to Google Calendar using Google's API
  • Authentication is handled through Google's secure OAuth infrastructure

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can revoke the app's Google Calendar access at any time through your Google Account settings
  • You can delete any calendar events created by the app directly in Google Calendar
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, which removes all local data

Security

We take security seriously:

  • All authentication is handled through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol
  • No passwords or credentials are stored by our application
  • Data transmission to Google Calendar uses secure HTTPS connections
  • The app follows Android security best practices

Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository at github.com/kianniek.
